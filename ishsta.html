League of Legends

League of Legends is a Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA) Game. It is a 5v5 game where the main objective is to slay enemy champions and destroy their base. Each team is constructed of 5 different positions, each position playing its own crucial role in the objective of the game. League includes over 160 unique champions to choose from to fit these roles.
