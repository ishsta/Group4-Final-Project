Kielbasa & Spinach Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 box of spaghetti 12 oz.
  • 1 kielbasa packet 12-14 oz.
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/8 cup chopped onions
  • 3-4 sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 cups of fresh spinach
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 3-4 slices of bacon
  • 1-2 tbsp of bacon grease from the bacon
  • Seasonings: salt, pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, red pepper flakes, and Original Mrs. Dash.
  • Squirt of lemon juice

    • Directions

  • Take 3 to 4 pieces of sun-dried tomatoes and soak them in water for 5 to 10 minutes.
  • Cook the bacon; when done, remove and leave the grease (should be 1-2 tablespoons).
  • Pour in chopped onions and saute on medium-low.
  • After the onions have softened, add two fresh minced garlic cloves.
  • Cut kielbasa into small slices and add to the pot.
  • Take the soaked sun-dried tomatoes and cut them into smaller pieces. Add to the mix. Increase heat to medium-high.
  • Take 2 tablespoons of butter and add to the kielbasa. Add the seasonings to taste.
  • (Approximately 1/8 a teaspoon is enough.)
  • While simmering, begin cooking the spaghetti for the allotted time on the box.
  • Rough cut spinach leaves, then add to kielbasa mixture. Stir.
  • When the pasta is ready, grab 1/3 cup of the pasta water to add to the kielbasa and spinach.
  • Drain the pasta and add the pasta to the pot.
  • Add the seasonings once more, and then add a small squirt of lemon juice to the mix.
  • Stir ingredients and let simmer for 3 to 5 minutes. Crumble the cooked bacon sliced into bits and add to the top.

    • Baked Mac-n-Cheese

    Ingredients:

  • 7 tbsp of butter
  • 1 1/2 cup of light cream
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp of pepper
  • 1/2 tsp of paprika
  • 2 cups of Mexican cheese blend
  • 1 1/2 cups of Italian cheese blend
  • 1 cup of Mozzarella cheese
  • Cheez-Its (crushed)
  • Potato Chips (crushed)
  • Box of macaroni 16 oz.

    • Directions

  • Preheat oven to 375. Begin cooking macaroni as directed by the box.
  • Grab a 5 quart, squared, casserole dish that can go in both microwave and oven and spray with cooking spray.
  • Place the 7 tablespoons of butter into the dish and pour in the 1 1/2 cups of light cream.
  • Place into the microwave until the butter is melted completely. Add the spices, then mix.
  • Pour in cheeses. Stir into cream mixture and return to microwave until the cheese is completely melted.
  • Once the macaroni is finished, add to cheese mixture and stir till completely combined.
  • Take the crushed Cheez-Its and Potato Chips place on top of macaroni in alternating layers. Do about two layers for each.
  • Place in the oven for 25-30 minutes. Let sit for 5 minutes before serving.

    • Vegetable Yummies

    Ingredients:

  • xZucchini
  • Summer squash
  • Cheese
  • Flour tortillas
  • Salsa or taco sauce
  • Frying oil
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder to taste.

    • Directions

  • Cut the zucchini and summer squash into small bite sized pieces.
  • Place into a medium to large saute pan and drizzle 2-3 tablespoons of olive oil over the squash.
  • Cook on medium-high heat. Season to taste.
  • In a small frying pan, pour in frying oil to just cover the bottom of the pan, deep enough to cover the bottom side of the flour tortilla.
  • Take one flour tortilla and add in a scoop of vegetables.
  • Place a spoonful of salsa or taco sauce, and sprinkle in cheese.
  • Fold the tortilla in half and place into frying plan.
  • Cook on both sides for 15-20 seconds or until the flour tortilla fluffs and becomes a crispy brown.
  • Remove from frying pan and let excess oil drain on a paper towel.
  • Pair with salsa, sour cream, and/or taco sauce.
